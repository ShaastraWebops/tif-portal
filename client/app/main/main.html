ABOUT TIF

Have you ever built something for a competition but don’t know what to do with it now? Do you have a working prototype of an idea which you think has the potential to go big but don’t have the means or the knowledge to take it forward? Are you looking to connect to Incubators or VCs for your project?


Don’t worry! We have exactly what you are looking for!


Tech and Innovation Fair (TIF) is one of the Flagship events of Shaastra 2018 which provides a platform to bring forward a hard core tech project or prototype and convert it into a minimum-viable product (MVP) with well-structured business mentoring that would ultimately ensure its success as a market-shelf product with a suitable target consumer pool.

WHY TIF

IIT Madras is slowly becoming a startup hub. Many noted startups have come out of the institute with the help of our Incubation Cell and other ample opportunities that IITM provides to its students.4 out of the Top 5 upcoming startups listed in the Economic Times Startup Awards 2017 were initially IIT Madras student projects that were successfully incubated into startups. This speaks volumes of the on-campus startup environment. Some of these student based startups are Detect, Hyperverge Inc., Ather Energy and Urban Ladder.


Our aim is to extend these opportunities to everyone out there looking to achieve something big. Through Tech and Innovation Fair, you are sure to get a glimpse of the startup environment of IIT Madras and also gain from it. So what are you waiting for? Go ahead and register!

HOW IT WORKS

The fair is conducted as a part of a competition which will have two online rounds and a finale at Shaastra 2018.

Stage 1 : Submission of project abstract, video and other required documents. This requires you to fill a simple form as part of the registration for the event.
[DEADLINE IS 10 NOVEMBER]

Stage 2 : Top 20 teams will be selected based on their respective submissions by a panel of experts in their respective fields. These teams will be provided full-fledged mentorship which will span an entire month including everything that you need to know about starting up. This will be followed by tasks and submissions which we are sure will help you learn a lot about what you can achieve through your project idea.

Stage 3 : A chance to showcase your project during Shaastra in front of thousands of people. This includes networking opportunities with various experts in the field during the event. The top 3 projects will be chosen by a panel.

Its a fair, so its going to be lot of fun.

PARTNERS

IITM Incubation Cell is the nodal body for all entrepreneurial activities arising from the wider IIT Madras community. Home to 120+ tech startups across sectors, many addressing national challenges through successful, self-sustaining businesses. One of India's leading incubators, IC aims to build and share resources including space and infrastructure, access to business support services, mentoring, training programmes to enhance the skills of entrepreneurs and seed funds.

E-CELL IITM is a student run non-profit organization that helps connect startups and entrepreneurs. It provides innovators with networking, mentorship support and incubation.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • The team should have a working hard core tech project or prototype that is, we are not looking for application or software based projects.
  • If your team has more than 4 members then please register (or send during Shaastra), a maximum of 4 people as representatives for the event
  • Every stage will have an evaluation procedure decided by experts and judges. Any decision by them will be final
  • All official communication will be done via email. It is the responsibility of the teams to check his/her email for any tasks assigned to them, if selected.
  • Should there be any discrepancies or issues, the decision taken by Shaastra shall be final and binding.
