About Us

Tech and Innovation Fair (TIF) provides a platform to bring forward a hard core tech project or prototype and convert it into a minimum-viable product (MVP) with well-structured business mentoring that would ultimately ensure its success as a market-shelf product with a suitable target consumer pool. The fair is conducted as a part of competition which will have an online round and a finale at IIT Madras during Shaastra 2019.

A selected number of applying teams will be screened forward to undergo mentorship from experts in the field of tech and business, which will assist them in building their project into a startup. With TIF, you will be networking with some of the top accelerators and incubators in India who will guide you through the right decisions and emerge successful in this highly competitive ecosystem.

At the final round you will be showcasing your product to the consumers and taking live feedback from them during Shaastra exhibition which witnesses a staggering footfall of 30,000 every year. The winners will receive a cash prize as well a chance to get incubated by some of India’s top firms.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

  1. Eligibility :

    2. The Competition is open to all currently enrolled students and early stage startups. Teams may include graduated students and the teams should have a hardware prototype while they are registering for the event. App based startups and services are not eligible to apply for the event. There is no limit to team size and interdisciplinary teams involving students from multiple colleges are strongly encouraged. The competition accepts as qualified teams new ventures or significant expansion of an existing business but only if the technology solution being proposed takes the venture in significantly different directions and promises major innovation and expansion. All team members should be present and participate in all webinars and workshops conducted online as well as during Shaastra. All plans, pitches, and presentations must be accurate and represent the venture in an honest manner. Misrepresentation will result in disqualification.

  2. Mentors :

    3. Mentors can be a valuable resource for startups and teams are encouraged to seek faculty, business, and technical mentors assigned to the them during the event. Feedbacks from their respective mentors shall be included in final evaluation of teams. You are strongly encouraged to develop solid business plan as well as an idea of your market b the end of online mentoring sessions with the help of our workshops and mentors.

  3. Judging :

    4. Each business plan and solutions developed during the course of event will be judged by a group of entrepreneurs, venture capitalists, and business professionals at the culmination of event. Ultimate ranking and awards are solely up to the discretion of the judging panel and your performance during mentoring sessions.

  4. Evaluation criteria :

    5. Shaastra Tech and Innovation Fair tries to simulate the process of entrepreneurship. Student entrepreneur teams should approach the competition as any new venture team would approach the marketplace. You are competing to solicit feedback from experienced entrepreneurs and business professionals and to gain start-up support and funding from potential investors. The judges will evaluate your business plans and technology as they would any business opportunity and then decide on the ventures they would most likely fund. Build your plan, pitch, and presentation with accordingly. The decisions of the judging panel are final and they are free to determine winners.

  5. Modification of Rules and Regulations :

    6. These guidelines cannot anticipate every circumstance. Consequently, the organizers reserves the right to modify these rules in any manner for the good of the competition. Any team that violates not only the rules and regulations but the spirit of the competition may be disqualified resulting in forfeiture of awards and prize money. Any questions regarding eligibility should be sent to the contact details provided in the website. We also reserve the right to use any information gathered during the course of this event for our future promotions and academic purpose. All affiliates and the organizers of the Competition retain the rights to use all competition materials to promote the events and for general academic purposes. Startups with no-disclosure clauses may please take note of this.
